# Final Experiment Stimuli

This folder contains the final Polish mnemonic images used in the evaluated experiment.

The final study focused on 10 Polish vocabulary items chosen for beginner-level learning and visual mnemonic suitability. Each item was paired with a generated mnemonic image, and in some conditions also with a short mnemonic explanation.

## Vocabulary items used

| Polish word | English meaning | Short mnemonic idea | Image file |
|---|---|---|---|
| dom | house | a dome-shaped house helps cue the sound of *dom* | dom.png |
| kot | cat | a cat wearing a coat helps cue the sound of *kot* | kot.png |
| okno | window | a broken window with an “oh no!” reaction helps cue *okno* | okno.png |
| pies | dog | a dog associated with pies helps cue *pies* | pies.png |
| ser | cheese | a “sir” linked to cheese helps cue *ser* | ser.png |
| zima | winter | a winter scene linked to “zip” or a similar sound cue helps cue *zima* | zima.png |
| sklep | shop/store | a shop scene linked with a skeleton cue helps cue *sklep* | sklep.png |
| stary | old | an old-looking star helps cue *stary* | stary.png |
| torba | bag | a torn bag helps cue *torba* | torba.png |
| ptak | bird | a bird about to attack helps cue *ptak* | ptak.png |

## Note
These images were the original stimuli used in the final experiment rather than the later improved examples discussed in the report.
