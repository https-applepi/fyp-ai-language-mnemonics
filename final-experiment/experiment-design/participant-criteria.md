# Participant Criteria

## Inclusion criteria

Participants included in the final study were required to have:

- little to no prior background in Polish
- completed both the immediate and delayed quizzes
- an anonymous participant ID that allowed Quiz 1 and Quiz 2 to be matched

## Why prior familiarity mattered

Participants were asked about their prior familiarity with Polish as part of recruitment. Only participants reporting little to no Polish background were included in the final study.

This was done to reduce prior-knowledge effects and make performance differences more attributable to the learning conditions rather than to existing vocabulary knowledge.

## Participant count

A total of 30 participants completed both quizzes and were included in the final dataset.

## Privacy and ethics

The study used anonymous participant IDs instead of names. No personally identifying participant information was required for the analysis.

The project was conducted as low-risk educational research using voluntary participation and anonymised response data.
