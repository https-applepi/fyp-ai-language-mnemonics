# Not-Chosen Stimuli List

This file lists the additional Polish vocabulary items generated during exploratory development but not selected for the final experiment. It briefly describes the mnemonic logic behind each stimulus.

## 1. słońce
- **English meaning:** sun
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *słońce* to “swan say” by showing a swan talking. The idea was intended as a pronunciation cue, but the connection to the true meaning, *sun*, is weak.
- **Image file:** `slonce_notchosen.png`

## 2. noga
- **English meaning:** leg
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *noga* to “no gap” by showing two legs with no gap between them. The visual supports the sound cue, but the association is slightly indirect.
- **Image file:** `noga_notchosen.png`

## 3. jabłko
- **English meaning:** apple
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *jabłko* to “yap call” by showing an apple talking on the phone. The cue is memorable, but the pronunciation link is somewhat stretched.
- **Image file:** `jablko_notchosen.png`

## 4. las
- **English meaning:** forest
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *las* to “lass” by showing a girl walking in a forest. While this creates a sound cue, it relies on a region-specific English word and was therefore not ideal.
- **Image file:** `las_notchosen.png`

## 5. droga
- **English meaning:** road
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *droga* to “drugs” by showing drugs on a road. Although it gives a sound-based cue, the imagery is inappropriate for a beginner learning resource.
- **Image file:** `droga_notchosen.png`

## 6. ogień
- **English meaning:** fire
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *ogień* to “oh yen” by showing yen on fire. The cue attempts to support pronunciation recall, but the imagery is unusual and less immediately meaningful.
- **Image file:** `ogien_notchosen.png`

## 7. ręka
- **English meaning:** hand
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *ręka* to “rank” by showing a hand with a rank tag on it. The hand remains visible, but the sound cue is not especially natural or strong.
- **Image file:** `reka_notchosen.png`

## 8. kawa
- **English meaning:** coffee
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *kawa* to *cava* by showing sparkling wine bubbling in a coffee cup. The cue is memorable, but it introduces an unsuitable alcohol-related reference.
- **Image file:** `kawa_notchosen.png`

## 9. woda
- **English meaning:** water
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *woda* to *vodka* by showing a vodka bottle pouring out water. Although the sound resemblance is strong, the alcohol reference made it unsuitable for inclusion.
- **Image file:** `woda_notchosen.png`

## 10. chleb
- **English meaning:** bread
- **Mnemonic idea:** The image links *chleb* to *club* by showing a man using a loaf of bread like a golf club. The bread remains present, but the scene is more silly than semantically clear.
